What is Just Notes

Just Notes is a small note-taking application designed to focus on the essentials of creating notes. That's it. Nothing more.

If you're looking for an app that has every feature imaginable, such as syncing to AWS, the ability to save audio recordings, themes, and syncing across devices, Just Notes is not the app for you.

However, if you want one that's responsive, easy to use, and designed to let you create your notes quickly and easily — even avoiding the need to use a mouse or touchpad — then Just Notes is the app for you. It's designed to be as unobtrusive and minimalist as possible, with a UX where every last detail is considered and debated.
